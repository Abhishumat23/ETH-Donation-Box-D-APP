How it works: Our donation dApp leverages Ethereum's Proof-of-Stake consensus mechanism where validators are chosen to propose and validate blocks based on their stake in the network.

‚úÖ Advantages for Donations

  • Energy Efficient: 99.95% less energy than Proof-of-Work
  • Lower Fees: Reduced transaction costs for donors
  • Faster Finality: Donations confirmed in ~12 seconds
  • Security: Economic penalties for malicious behavior
  • Decentralization: No single point of failure

‚ö†Ô∏è Considerations

  • Complexity: Requires understanding of staking
  • Initial Barrier: 32 ETH minimum to be a validator
  • Slashing Risk: Validators can lose stake for misbehavior
  • Rich Get Richer: Higher stakes = more rewards
